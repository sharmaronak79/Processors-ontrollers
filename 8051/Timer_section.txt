Why timer?
For delay

Two types:-
1. Software delay
2. Hardware delay

Software delay easy and convenient,  operated by processors 


Hardware delay uses extra hardware, high cost compare to software delay

But increase the speed as it keeps processor free from execute the delay function like loop or no operation functiln


Two SFR : TCON TMOD

TCON: TF1 TF0 TR1 TRO IE1 IE0 IT1 IT0

TMOD : GATE1 C/T'1 M1 MO GATE2 C/T'0 M1 MO
